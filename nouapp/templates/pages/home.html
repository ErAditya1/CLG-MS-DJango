üè´ About Adarsh Inter College

Adarsh Inter College, situated in Usmanpur, Barabanki, Uttar Pradesh, is a respected educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values.

Founded with the mission of providing quality education to rural and semi-urban students, the college has become a cornerstone of learning and development in the region.

The college was established under the vision and guidance of Shri Vidya Prakash, the Sansthapak (Founder), and is efficiently managed by Shri Dinesh Sharma, the Prabandhak (Manager).

It offers intermediate-level education supported by experienced faculty and modern facilities, emphasizing academic excellence and character building.

With a legacy of service and a vision for the future, Adarsh Inter College remains committed to empowering students and contributing to societal progress.
