üè´ About Adarsh Inter College

Adarsh Inter College, situated in Usmanpur, Barabanki, Uttar Pradesh, is a respected educational institution dedicated to nurturing young minds with knowledge, discipline, and strong moral values. Founded with the mission of providing quality education to rural and semi-urban students, the college has become a cornerstone of learning and development in the region.

The college was established under the vision and guidance of Shri Vidya Prakash, the Sansthapak (Founder), whose commitment to education continues to inspire its growth. The institution is efficiently managed by Shri Dinesh Sharma, the Prabandhak (Manager), whose leadership ensures smooth administration and the ongoing upliftment of academic standards.

Adarsh Inter College offers intermediate-level education in a supportive environment, guided by experienced faculty and supported by modern facilities. The college emphasizes both academic excellence and character building, helping students prepare for higher education, careers, and responsible citizenship.

With a legacy of service and a vision for the future, Adarsh Inter College remains committed to empowering students and contributing to the progress of society.
